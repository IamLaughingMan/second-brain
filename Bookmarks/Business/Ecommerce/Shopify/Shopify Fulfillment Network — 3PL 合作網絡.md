## Why I saved this

Shopify 自己嘅 fulfillment 策略 reference：佢**唔自營倉**（2023 Q2 賣咗 Deliverr／Shopify Logistics 畀 Flexport 之後改做撮合 model），呢個 page 列齊 currently 嘅 8 個 partner 3PL，做 vendor-due-diligence baseline。

## Summary

Shopify Fulfillment Network = **partner-marketplace 模式**（非 Shopify 自營倉）：撮合 3PL 合作夥伴管庫存／發貨／retail-near positioning，Shopify admin 做 single pane of glass（庫存／shipment／returns／performance）。

3 步流程：① 比較 partner pricing／capability／metrics、揀一個 → ② 寄存貨去 partner 倉、sync data → ③ 喺 Shopify admin oversee。

## Key Takeaways

### 8 個 partner 3PL（2026-06-21 snapshot）

| 3PL | 賣點 | 規模／門檻 |
|-----|------|-----------|
| **Flexport** ⭐ **Preferred** | End-to-end logistics（唯一 truly E2E provider） | — |
| **Amazon Fulfillment** | 借 Amazon 全球網絡，~97%+ on-time；100+ integrations | 跨 channel single inventory pool |
| **Bigblue** | Omnichannel／DTC+B2B branded experience | EU 9 倉、全球 carrier 網 |
| **DHL Fulfillment Network** | 利用 DHL local+global 經驗 | **≥ 3,000 orders/月** 起 |
| **GoBolt** | 強調 sustainability | — |
| **Mayple Global** | 1 hub 直發 **80+ 國**，managed duties/tax/CX | DTC 無需開海外倉 |
| **ShipBob** | Global omnichannel + full-stack software | **60+ 倉** 全球 |
| **Shipfusion** | Owned warehouses、dedicated account manager、custom flow | 「boutique 3PL」定位 |

## My Notes

- **歷史背景**：Shopify 喺 2023 年 Q2 將自家嘅 fulfillment 業務（Deliverr / Shopify Logistics）整盤賣畀 Flexport，從此改做 **撮合 model**——所以 Flexport 仍係「preferred partner」屬 legacy 關係。背景值得 SMB 商戶留意（vendor concentration risk）。
- **DHL 3K orders/月 門檻** = 中小型賣家篩選器；起步小於呢個量要揾其他 partner。
- **Mayple Global「1 hub 80 國」** model 喺紙面好抵，但 cross-border duty/CX 細節仲係要 case-by-case 評估。
- **ShipBob 60+ 倉 vs Bigblue 9 EU 倉** = 全球 vs 區域強項對比，揀邊個睇 target market 集中度。
- **Page 性質**：純 sales/marketing landing，**冇定價**——真正比較要入 Shopify admin 攞 quotes。
- **無留言區概念**（B2B landing page）。

## Ship faster, reach further

Shopify's 3PL partners manage inventory, fulfill and ship orders, and position products closer to customers—enhancing your entire supply chain.

### Focus on growth, not logistics

Scale your brand and expand your customer base without having to deal with inventory, packing, and shipping.

### Lean on experts to reduce costs

Save money with volume-based shipping discounts and eliminate the overhead of managing your own fulfillment.

### Enhance customer experience

Ship from fulfillment centers close to your customers so they get deliveries faster.

## How Shopify Fulfillment Network works

### Connect with a 3PL partner

Compare our trusted partners for pricing estimates, detailed capabilities, and performance metrics—then connect your choice to your store admin.

### Send inventory and sync data

Send products to your new partner's fulfillment centers, where they'll be closer to buyers, and deliver to their door faster.

### Oversee fulfillment on Shopify

Monitor shipments, inventory levels, returns, deliveries, business performance, and more from one place.

## Meet our trusted fulfillment partners

Logistics experts in Shopify Fulfillment Network are handpicked for their ability to scale your business.

**Preferred partner — Flexport**: Flexport is the only logistics provider that offers a truly end-to-end solution. They enable customers to manage a single pool of inventory, sell in more places with a single click, and deliver right to a customer's doorstep at lightning speed.

**Amazon**: Leverage the speed and reliability of Amazon's global fulfillment network to ensure fast, reliable shipping—up to 97%+ on time. With over 100 integrations and a single pool of inventory, fulfillment is simplified across all sales channels.

**Bigblue**: The all-in-one fulfillment platform for omnichannel brands. With 9 EU warehouses, a large global carrier network, and advanced technology, Bigblue enables complex DTC & B2B operations and delivers best-in-class branded customer experiences at scale.

**DHL Fulfillment Network**: enables you to scale your business efficiently, starting from 3,000 orders a month. Leverage our solid experience and extensive local and global network to support your growth.

**GoBolt**: exists to simplify logistics. They handle everything from receiving and storing your inventory to delivering packages to your customers, and they're doing it sustainably. Let GoBolt manage your logistics while you grow your brand.

**Mayple Global**: lets DTC brands expand worldwide—no need for costly warehouses. One hub delivers fast, affordable shipping to 80+ countries, with managed duties, taxes, and customer experience, making global fulfillment easy and profitable.

**ShipBob**: a global omnichannel fulfillment solution with best-in-class technology and operations through full-stack software and 60+ fulfillment centers around the world.

**Shipfusion**: With owned warehouses, dedicated account managers, and custom fulfillment flows, Shipfusion is built for your growth, providing international coverage with the bespoke service of a boutique 3PL.
