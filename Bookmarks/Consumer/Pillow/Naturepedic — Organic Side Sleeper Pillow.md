## WHAT MAKES THIS ORGANIC LATEX PILLOW SO SUPPORTIVE?

## CURVED SIDE-SLEEPER DESIGN

Unlike a standard pillow shape, the Organic Latex Side Sleeper Pillow is intentionally contoured to better accommodate the side-sleeping position. Its curved design helps to create space for the shoulder while supporting the head and neck at a more comfortable angle. This creates a pillow that feels like it fits your body instead of fighting against it.

## TARGETED NECK SUPPORT

Proper neck support is especially important for side sleepers, since the distance between the head and mattress tends to be greater in this position. This pillow is designed to help bridge that gap with a supportive yet adaptable feel. The combination of curved construction and resilient latex fill helps to cradle the neck and shoulders while encouraging healthier alignment throughout the night.

## ADJUSTABLE LOFT

Not every side sleeper needs the same amount of loft. Shoulder width, body type and mattress feel all affect what is most comfortable. That is why this pillow includes easy zipper access to the interior, allowing you to add or remove fill until the loft feels just right. Whether you prefer a fuller feel or slightly lower profile, you can personalize your support instead of settling for one fixed height.

## GOLS-CERTIFIED LATEX

Find the comfort and support you want without the trade-offs of memory foam. Unlike memory, soy or “eco” foams, which are all types of polyurethane foam, our GOLS-certified organic latex offers a healthier, naturally resilient sleep surface. It responds quickly to movement, provides buoyant support and helps the pillow to maintain its shape over time.

## NATURAL PRESSURE RELIEF

The pillow’s shredded organic latex fill gently contours while maintaining responsive support. Latex compresses under pressure but quickly rebounds, helping it cushion pressure points around the head, neck and shoulders without collapsing overnight. This balance of contouring comfort and resilient support can help side sleepers to settle in more comfortably and wake up with less stiffness.

## SLEEPS COOLER

Aside from chemical concerns, memory foam is notorious for trapping heat. Latex, on the other hand, has a more breathable structure that allows for better airflow and temperature regulation. We take that a step further with shredded latex fill and a breathable organic cotton encasement to help create a cooler, fresher sleep experience.

#### FAQs

#### What type of pillow is best for side sleepers?

Side sleepers generally do best with a pillow that offers enough loft to fill the space between the head and mattress while supporting the neck in a more neutral position. A pillow with responsive support and a shape designed specifically for side sleeping can help to [improve alignment](https://www.naturepedic.com/blog/healthiest-sleeping-position) and reduce pressure around the neck and shoulders.

#### Will this pillow work if I switch between side and back sleeping?

Yes, it can. While the Organic Latex Side Sleeper Pillow is optimized for side sleeping, the adjustable fill makes it easier to customize the loft for combination sleepers who occasionally shift onto their back. Side sleepers will benefit most from its curved design, but the flexibility can work well for mixed sleep styles too.

#### How should side sleepers adjust their pillow fill?

Start by sleeping on the pillow as it arrives, then make small adjustments based on how your neck and shoulders feel. If your head feels pushed too high, remove a bit of fill. If you need more support, add some back in. The goal is to create a loft that helps keep your head, neck and spine more comfortably aligned.

#### Will this side sleeper pillow keep its shape overnight?

The shredded latex fill is designed to be resilient and responsive, helping the pillow to maintain its loft and support better than many conventional fiber-filled pillows. Regular fluffing can help preserve shape, and the latex fill is less prone to flattening over time than many synthetic alternatives.

#### Is latex better than memory foam for side sleepers?

Many side sleepers prefer latex because it provides responsive support without the sinking feeling of memory foam – it springs back as you move. Latex is also more breathable, especially shredded or perforated latex, while memory foam can trap heat due to its dense structure. Additionally, most memory foam is made with polyurethane foam and other chemical additives, whereas natural latex pillows are derived largely from rubber tree sap.

#### Can the pillow cover be washed?

No. The Organic Side Sleeper Pillow is spot clean only. We recommend using a [pillow protector](https://www.naturepedic.com/organic-cotton-waterproof-pillow-protector) to keep your pillow clean and extend its lifespan.
