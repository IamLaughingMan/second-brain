## What is Pillow Height (also called Pillow Loft)?

The measurement of cushions from top to bottom called pillow loft functions as an essential requirement to maintain spinal alignment during rest especially for persons who sleep on their side. The measurement to determine a pillow's thickness after flat set up provides its loft definition.

Side sleepers need proper pillow fitting to achieve safe spinal and neck alignment which avoids unwanted discomfort in the body. Proper alignment between pillow height and body position functions as a vital factor in reducing neck shoulder pain so it does not affect sleep quality or health outcomes.

### Understanding Pillow Loft

Pillow loft can be categorized into three main types: low, medium, and high.

- #### Low Loft:
	Typically less than 3 inches tall. The pillows function well for people who sleep on their stomach yet fail to support side sleepers properly thus creating neck tension.
- #### Medium Loft:
	The product stands at different heights between 7.5 to 12.7 centimeters. The suitable elevation of this height works well for most side sleepers since it strikes an optimal balance between support and comfort. The proper spinal alignment remains unchanged when using a pillow of medium height that does not become cumbersome.
- #### High Loft:
	The taller than 5 inches height of these pillows offers ideal support for side sleeping individuals with broad shoulders who struggle to fill the distance between their bed and their head. Your sleeping head will receive perfect support from a pillow with a high loft that prevents undesirable curves in your neck.

## FAQs

### A) What is the ideal pillow thickness?

A stomach sleeper will do best with a low pillow of up to 3 inches; back sleepers prefer medium pillows ranging from 3 to 5 inches thick; and side sleepers need high pillows of up to 5 inches thick.

### B) What pillow should a side sleeper use?

Your pillow should contour to your sleeping position, so you should decide if you need a firmer or softer pillow. Soft, tall pillows are best for side sleepers, “tall” meaning height in pillow lingo.

### C) Is using thick pillows when sleeping preferable?

The answer varies for each person in particular. Generally selling, side sleepers are advised to use a thicker pillow since it fills the void between the neck, shoulders, and the mattress.

### D) What signs indicate that my pillow is excessively thick?

Very thick pillows can be problematic since they may cause neck strain due to their lack of motion. The perfect pillow should cushion the head while allowing the sleeper to comfortably rest without forcing them to fit its shape.

### E) How many pillows is a side sleeper supposed to use?

Two are necessary, but it is possible to get by with just one high-quality pillow placed under the head.

### F) How can I use a pillow for a side sleeper?

For side sleepers, the pillow should go from under the arms to the curve of the neck and to the top of the head.
