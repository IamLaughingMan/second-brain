## SLEEPS COOL

Our molded latex pillow is designed for cool sleeping, thanks to its breathable, molded latex core. This material **naturally promotes air circulation**, preventing heat buildup throughout the night. Additionally, the pillow's perforated structure enhances this cooling effect by allowing air to flow freely, ensuring a comfortable and cool sleep environment.

## FIRMLY SUPPORTIVE MOLDED LATEX CORE

Our molded charcoal-infused latex core is designed for those who need more support, functionality, and comfort from their pillow. It’s **ideal for side- and back-sleepers.** To make the pillow, we pour FSC-certified latex into a mold that is the shape of the pillow and steam-bake it. This makes a uniquely firm and supportive core that **provides gentle, buoyant, firm support for your neck and head**.

## CHARCOAL POWER

Charcoal, sourced from rice husks, is a natural and versatile material ideal for pillows due to its temperature regulation, moisture, and odor control properties. Infused into the latex before vulcanization, it spreads evenly throughout the core of our Molded Latex Pillow, creating a **supportive and cool** pillow that **combats body odors and moisture**.
