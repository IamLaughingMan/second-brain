## adjustable comfort & support

With the Organic Adjustable Latex Pillow, we put YOU in control of how much fill you want in your pillow (we even include an extra bag!). This allows you to adjust the loft and optimize your pillow for better spinal alignment. The pillow features premium GOLS or FSC® certified latex that has been **precisely cut into small pieces** and blended with approximately 10% organic cotton fiber. These pieces create space around them, promoting air flow throughout the pillow while maintaining just the right density and resilience. This is superior to simply shredding the latex, which results in a denser consistency with reduced airflow and loft. Designed in a way that maximizes personalization and flexibility, you get the comfort you deserve for your best sleep yet!

## removable, washable outer cover

The luxurious organic cotton outer cover is soft and stretchy so it can perfectly contour to your head and neck while remaining easy to slip off for laundering. Since the latex is fully contained in an internal organic cotton jersey encasement, there is no mess when removing the outer cover. This practical yet elegant approach makes it simple to care for your pillow, keeping it fresh and clean for years to come. Machine wash and dry.
