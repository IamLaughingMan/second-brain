# Laureys 2014 — Water Kefir Microbial Diversity & Metabolite Kinetics

## Why I saved this

Water kefir 發酵嘅 reference-grade 研究：第一次喺 192h 內 systematically 追蹤微生物群、代謝物 kinetics、aroma compounds，**Lb. harbinensis 首次喺水克菲爾鑑出**；做家庭 brewing／probiotic 文獻 baseline 必收。

## Summary

VUB（Vrije Universiteit Brussel）研究組用 **multiphasic approach**（culture-dependent + culture-independent PCR-DGGE + 代謝物 HPLC/GC-MS）追蹤一個 192h water kefir 發酵：

- **微生物群**穩定喺整個 fermentation（液體與 grain 兩相 species 一致；密度 grain 高 10–30 倍）
- **蔗糖 24h 內耗盡**，同步產 grain polysaccharide（glucan）
- **代謝主軸 72h 完成**：ethanol（20.3 g/L）+ lactic acid（4.9 g/L）+ acetic（1.0）+ glycerol（2.3）+ mannitol（0.8）
- **pH 4.26 → 3.45（72h）→ 3.35（192h）**
- **Acetic acid bacteria 144h 後先 detectable**——標準 72h 家用發酵期內**冇 AAB 顯著貢獻**
- **Carbon recovery 100.6%** — 質量平衡完整

## Key Takeaways

### 1. 微生物 species panel（識別出嘅核心 6 種）

**Lactic Acid Bacteria（細菌部分，依優勢序）：**
- **Lactobacillus casei / paracasei** — 兼性異發酵；oral / gut / dairy / vegetable 都有；部分株具 probiotic 潛力
- **Lactobacillus hilgardii** — 嚴格異發酵；wine／cocoa fermentation 都有；**主要 EPS（外多醣）生產者**
- **Lactobacillus harbinensis** — 兼性異發酵；**首次喺 water kefir 鑑出**；產**抗真菌化合物**會抑制 yeast
- **Lactobacillus nagelii** — 同型發酵；常見 water kefir 種
- **Lactobacillus hordei / mali** — 同樣常見
- **Bifidobacterium psychraerophilum / crudilactis** — 嚴格厭氧；典型產 acetate > lactate；本研究 acetate 量低 = 代謝活性受限
- **Acetobacter lovaniensis / fabarum** — 144h 後先出現，typical 72h 發酵冇佢份

**Yeast：**
- **Saccharomyces cerevisiae**（主導）
- **Dekkera bruxellensis**（anamorph: Brettanomyces bruxellensis）—— 比利時酸啤主角、wine/beer spoilage 常見；對 water kefir 嘅 net effect 仲未明

### 2. 群落動態

- **Grain = 微生物 reservoir**：cell density 比 liquor 高 **10–30 倍**；total cells 喺 grain 上比 liquor 多 **4–10 倍**
- **Lactic Acid Bacteria : Yeast 比例 ≈ 2:1–10:1**（穩定）
- **pH < 3.5 抑制 enterococci / Enterobacteriaceae** —— 食品安全 implication：低 pH 自然 protective

### 3. 代謝 kinetics（72h 內完成大部分）

| 時間 | Sucrose (g/L) | Ethanol (g/L) | Lactic (g/L) | pH |
|------|--------------|--------------|--------------|-----|
| 0h | 47.5 | 1.1 | 0.7 | 4.26 |
| 24h | 1.2 | — | — | — |
| 72h | ~0 | 20.3 | 4.9 | 3.45 |
| 192h | 0 | — | — | 3.35 |

- **Grain mass** 由 16.4g 增到 28.6g（24h）—— 主要靠 sucrose-driven polysaccharide 生產
- **Mannitol** 由 fructose（heterofermentative LAB）；微量但**有抗氧化活性 + 甜味**，desirable for water kefir
- **Glycerol**（yeast 產）—— 微甜、增黏度、對風味影響細

### 4. 香氣化合物（72h 取樣）

**主要 yeast-origin esters**（按相對 threshold 倍數排）：

| 化合物 | 濃度 (mg/L) | Threshold (mg/L) | 倍數 | 描述 |
|--------|-----------|------------------|------|------|
| **Ethyl octanoate** | 3.44 | 0.005 | **688×** | 鳳梨、梨、果香 |
| **Ethyl hexanoate** | 0.37 | 0.014 | 26× | 蘋果、香蕉、紫羅蘭 |
| **Isoamyl acetate** | 0.11 | 0.03 | 3.7× | 甜、香蕉、梨 |
| **Ethyl decanoate** | 1.40 | 0.2 | 7× | 花香 |
| Ethyl acetate | 13.4 | 7.5 | 1.8× | 果香（高量會有指甲油味） |
| Isoamyl alcohol | 44.1 | 30 | 1.5× | 強烈、不愉快 |
| 2-Methyl-1-propanol | 11.6 | 40 | 0.3× | 酒精／燃料 |

→ **主導風味 = 4 種 esters**（果香花香），其他 alcohol 量大但 threshold 高、感官貢獻細

### 5. 結論

- 三大主菌：Lb. casei/paracasei + Lb. harbinensis + Lb. hilgardii
- 兩大主酵母：S. cerevisiae + D. bruxellensis
- LAB > yeast 數量，但**yeast 代謝主導**（產 ethanol+CO2）
- Grain mass 增長與蔗糖 supply 同步、polysaccharide **唔係 reserve polymer**（不會回降）
- Major end products：ethanol、CO2、lactic acid、glycerol、mannitol、acetic acid

## My Notes

- **本 paper 嘅 significance**：第一份系統 multiphasic 分析 water kefir 嘅 community dynamics + metabolite kinetics + aroma。之前文獻多單分析 species diversity 或 grain polysaccharide 結構，未做 time-series。
- **家用發酵 implication**：
  - **24h 後蔗糖已耗盡** → 想避高酒精（>20 g/L = ~2.5% ABV）可以 ≤ 24h sieve；想酸性更強就放足 72h
  - **144h 後 AAB 出現** → 過久發酵會偏向 vinegar-like
  - **二次發酵加水果** → 24-72h 之間最 active、可助 carbonation
- **Lb. casei 嘅 probiotic 潛力 mentioned**：作者明示 "novel probiotic Lb. casei strains" 可從 water kefir 篩，呢個係**核心 selling point** 之一（不過 strain-specific，唔等於飲水克菲爾 = 攝取 probiotics）。
- **D. bruxellensis 嘅模糊**：作者誠實寫 "unclear" 對 end product 影響；wine/beer 行業視之為 spoilage、Belgian Lambic／Geuze 視之為核心—— water kefir 屬中間區。
- **Limitation**：
  - 單一 inoculum 來源（一個 Belgian 私人家庭）—— **唔同 origin 嘅 water kefir 物種組成可能差異大**（同一文獻組 Gulitz 2011 已示）。
  - 21°C 標準 lab 條件 vs 家用變溫；溫度影響菌相平衡
  - 干 fig 喺 inoculum / medium 中 —— 唔同 sweetener／添加（葡萄乾 vs 干 fig）會 shift 結果
- **可對標**：
  - **Milk kefir**（不同 grain matrix、kefiran）：菌相重疊但 yeast 偏 Kluyveromyces；可搜「Pidoux」「Stadie」「Marsh」review
  - **Tibicos / Tibi** = 同物（地方名）
  - **Kombucha** = 紅茶醋；SCOBY 主體係 Acetobacter（呢度只 144h 後先出現）+ Komagataeibacter，菌相分別大

### Abstract

Water kefir is a sour, alcoholic, and fruity fermented beverage of which the fermentation is started with water kefir grains. These water kefir grains consist of polysaccharide and contain the microorganisms responsible for the water kefir fermentation. In this work, a water kefir fermentation process was followed as a function of time during 192 h to unravel the community dynamics, the species diversity, and the kinetics of substrate consumption and metabolite production. The majority of the water kefir ecosystem was found to be present on the water kefir grains. The most important microbial species present were *Lactobacillus casei* / *paracasei*, *Lactobacillus harbinensis*, *Lactobacillus hilgardii*, *Bifidobacterium psychraerophilum* / *crudilactis*, *Saccharomyces cerevisiae*, and *Dekkera bruxellensis*. The microbial species diversities in the water kefir liquor and on the water kefir grains were similar and remained stable during the whole fermentation process. The major substrate, sucrose, was completely converted after 24 h of fermentation, which coincided with the production of the major part of the water kefir grain polysaccharide. The main metabolites of the fermentation were ethanol and lactic acid. Glycerol, acetic acid, and mannitol were produced in low concentrations. The major part of these metabolites was produced during the first 72 h of fermentation, during which the pH decreased from 4.26 to 3.45. The most prevalent volatile aroma compounds were ethyl acetate, isoamyl acetate, ethyl hexanoate, ethyl octanoate, and ethyl decanoate, which might be of significance with respect to the aroma of the end product.

### INTRODUCTION（核心）

Water kefir is a fermented beverage that is made by adding water kefir grains, which are polysaccharide grains that serve as the inoculum, to a mixture of water, sugar (sucrose), dried figs, and possibly other ingredients such as lemon, depending on the recipe. After 2 to 4 days of anaerobic incubation at room temperature, a sparkling, yellowish fermented beverage is obtained that has a fruity, acidic, slightly sweet, and slightly alcoholic taste and aroma. Water kefir is available worldwide, but it is still unknown what the real origin of the water kefir grains is. It has been postulated that the polysaccharide grains originate from the leaves of the *Opuntia* cactus fig plant.

**Aliases by region**：water kefir grains, ginger beer plants, Tibicos, Tibi grains, California bees, African bees, ale nuts, balm of Gilead, Bèbées, Japanese beer seeds, sugary kefir grains.

It is known that the microbial species diversity of water kefir consists of a stable consortium of mainly **lactic acid bacteria, yeasts, and acetic acid bacteria**. Recently, *Bifidobacterium psychraerophilum / crudilactis* was also found. Different water kefirs display different species diversities. The water kefir grain polysaccharide consists of **dextran, an α-(1→6)-linked glucose polymer**, produced by certain *Lactobacillus* and/or *Leuconostoc* species.

**Aim**：gain a deeper understanding of the water kefir fermentation process — microbial species diversity, community dynamics, substrate consumption profile, and metabolite production course.

### MATERIALS AND METHODS（要點）

- **Inoculum**：~100g grains from a private cultivator (Ghent, Belgium); pre-fermented in series of 1L→2L→5L Schott bottles to ~600g; 21°C water bath; 72h backslopping
- **Fermentation**：triplicate, 100mL Schott bottles with water lock; 15g grains + 85mL simulation medium (6g unrefined cane sugar + 65mL tap water + 20mL fig extract); 21°C; sampling at 0, 3, 6, 12, 18, 24, 36, 48, 72, 96, 144, 192 h
- **Microbial enumeration**：MRS (LAB), mDMS (AAB), YG (yeast) agar; KAA + VRBG for enterococci/streptococci + Enterobacteriaceae
- **Culture-dependent ID**：(GTG)5-PCR fingerprinting (bacteria), M13-PCR (yeast), 16S rRNA / ITS / LSU rRNA sequencing
- **Culture-independent**：PCR-DGGE with V3 (universal), LAC (LAB-specific), Bif (bifidobacteria), yeast primer pairs
- **Substrate / metabolites**：HPAEC-PAD (sugars, glycerol, mannitol), HPLC-RI (lactic / acetic acid), GC-FID (ethanol)
- **Volatile aroma**：SH-GC-MS（static headspace + mass spec）
- **Carbon recovery**：mass balance check

### RESULTS（要點）

**Grain mass / pH**：grain wet mass 16.4 → 28.6 g (24h)，之後穩定；dry mass 16.7% → 13–14%；pH 4.85（medium）→ 4.26（加 inoculum）→ 3.45（72h）→ 3.35（192h）

**Enumerations**：LAB 8.2 / yeast 7.4 log CFU/g grain；LAB 6.9 / yeast 6.3 log CFU/mL liquor；AAB 144h 後先 quantifiable；enterococci / Enterobacteriaceae 0（低 pH 抑制）

**Species（culture-dependent）**：bacteria — Lb. casei/paracasei, Lb. hilgardii, Lb. harbinensis, Lb. nagelii, Acetobacter lovaniensis/fabarum, Lb. hordei/mali（按優勢序）；yeasts — S. cerevisiae, D. bruxellensis

**Species（culture-independent PCR-DGGE）**：confirmed above + B. psychraerophilum/crudilactis（Bif primer）

**Substrate / metabolites**：sucrose 47.5 → 1.2 g/L（24h）；total carbs 75.1 → 3.1 g/L（72h）；ethanol 1.1 → 20.3 g/L（72h，linear increase）；lactic 0.7 → 4.9；acetic 0.1 → 1.0；glycerol → 2.3；mannitol → 0.8

**Aroma**（72h）：ethyl octanoate 688× threshold；ethyl hexanoate 26×；ethyl decanoate 7×；isoamyl acetate 3.7×；ethyl acetate 1.8× → 主導 fruity/floral notes

**Carbon recovery**：100.6%（mass balance complete）

### DISCUSSION（重點）

- LAB / yeast / bifidobacteria / AAB are core; **grain harbours the microorganisms**（density 高 10–30 倍）
- **Lb. harbinensis 首次喺 water kefir 鑑出**——之前見於中國蔬菜發酵、French cow milk、口腔生態、Parmigiano Reggiano、sorghum sourdough；**產抗真菌化合物抑制酵母**
- **Lb. hilgardii** 一般係 main EPS producer，但非所有 strain 都產；其他 EPS 候選：Leuconostoc mesenteroides, Lb. brevis, Lb. casei, Lb. nagelii, Lb. hordei
- **Sucrose 係 polysaccharide synthesis 必需基質**（grain 只喺 sucrose 在場時長）
- **B. psychraerophilum**（首離自 porcine cecum）—— 厭氧、產 acetate > lactate；本研究 acetate 量低反映其活性受限
- **D. bruxellensis** 喺 Belgian acid ale（Lambic / Geuze）係核心、wine / beer 行業多視為 spoilage —— water kefir 屬「unclear net effect」
- **Mannitol（fructose → mannitol via 異發酵 LAB）**：sweet + antioxidant，desirable
- **Esters**（isoamyl acetate, ethyl hexanoate / octanoate / decanoate）= yeast 代謝產物，與 wine/beer 共通；主導 water kefir 嘅 fruity/floral 香氣
