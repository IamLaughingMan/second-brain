## Why I saved this

Hair re-pigmentation 領域嘅**化妝品工業專利示例**：呢個 patent 同 [[Sumitomo 2020 — Dupilumab Reverses Hair Graying (J Dermatol Letter)|Sumitomo 2020 dupilumab → 復黑]] case report 屬同主題、但**完全不同 mechanism**（biologic 抗炎 vs 局部抗氧化），值得對讀。**EUK-134** 係 cosmetic / longevity 圈知名嘅 catalase/SOD mimetic，呢個 patent 顯示 Henkel 曾認真探索其商業化用途。

## Summary

**Invention**：cosmetic 配方含 **EUK-134** 同／或 **EUK-118**（兩者都係 anti-oxidative manganese-salen 複合物），加上至少一種「care substance」（護理成分），可影響皮膚／頭髮嘅天然 pigmentation 過程 —— 即**抑制／反轉灰髮**。

**Mechanism rationale**（patent 自述）：
- 灰髮 = hair follicle melanin synthesis 減少
- 關鍵分子網絡：**MCR1**（melanocortin receptor 1）／**gp100**（melanosomal membrane protein）／**c-kit**（SCF receptor）／α-MSH／SCF
- Hair follicle melanocyte 受 oxidative stress 殺死或 melanogenesis 抑制 → 變灰
- EUK-134／EUK-118 嘅 SOD/catalase-mimetic 活性 → 清除自由基／H₂O₂ → 保護 melanocyte／維持 melanogenesis
- 引述 prior art WO 2003/103616：抗氧化 mimic EUK-8 已知保護 melanocyte 抗 TNF-α／TGF-β／IL-1β 等 cytotoxic agents

**Preferred formulation**：

- EUK-134 ／ EUK-118 含量：**0.001 – 1 wt%**（最 preferred 區間）
- 範圍可擴：0.000001–10 wt% / 0.00001–5 wt% / 0.0001–2 wt%
- 載體：「physiologically acceptable carrier」
- 至少一種 care substance：cationic／amphoteric polymers／plant extracts／vitamins／ubiquinones／bisabolol／taurine／silicones／urea／creatine／ectoine／α-OH-acids 等
- 產品形式：shampoo / conditioner / hair tonic / spray / gel / dye / wax / mousse / styling / mask

## Key Takeaways

### EUK-134 是乜（化學）

- **EUK-134 = Chloro[[2,2'-[1,2-ethanediylbis[(nitrilo-κN)methylidyne]]bis[6-methoxyphenolato-κO]]]-manganese**
- 別名：ethyl bisiminoethyl guaiacol manganese complex；EBG-Mn
- **由 Eukarion Inc.（已 acquired）** 開發；其他知名 EUK 系列：**EUK-8**、**EUK-118**、**EUK-189**
- 化學本質：**Mn(III) salen** 複合物
- 生物活性：**SOD（超氧化物歧化酶） + catalase 雙重活性 mimic**（synthetic 模擬內源酶）

### EUK-118 是乜

- **EUK-118 = (acetato-κO)[[2,2'-[1,2-ethanediylbis[(nitrilo-κN)-methylidyne]bis[3,5-dimethoxyphenolato-κO]](2-)]manganese**
- 同 EUK-134 結構相似（多兩個 methoxy）；同類 anti-oxidative 性質

cationic／amphoteric polymers、plant extracts、vitamins／pro-vitamins、carbohydrates、quinones（particularly **ubiquinone/CoQ10**）、bisabolol、bisaboloxides、taurine、silicones、furanones、herbal oils、urea / urea derivatives、natural betaines、creatine、ectoine、α-hydroxy carboxylic acid（AHA）／α-ketocarboxylic acid／β-hydroxycarboxylic acid（BHA）／其 ester／lactone／salt form、fatty acid triglycerides、flavonoids／isoflavonoids

1. **Henkel 主動放棄**（commercial value 唔夠 cover maintenance fees）

**對使用者意義**：呢個 invention 嘅 IP 保護**已不存在於大部分 jurisdictions**（除非 Henkel 喺特定國家有 granted national patent）。即係：**EUK-134 / EUK-118 用於 hair pigmentation cosmetics 嘅 method／composition，可能已喺 most markets 屬 public domain**——但**用嚟做產品前自己再 verify** national patent status（Espacenet／PATENTSCOPE 查 family members）。

### Cosmetic 真正商品化情況

睇市場：

- **L'Oréal / La Roche-Posay** 等：有用 **catalase enzymes**（true biocatalyst）做去灰髮（如 Heliocare／Greyverse 等）—— mechanism similar
- **EUK-134 本身**：仲未見大型品牌主打嘅 mass-market hair product；多用於 **anti-aging skin care**（如 Mibelle Biochemistry 嘅 PrimaQuench）—— Mibelle 有 EUK-134 commercial supply chain
- **Henkel 自家旗下 Schwarzkopf / Joico**：未見專門 EUK-134 hair re-pigmentation 產品 launch ── 可能 R&D 結果未 commercially viable（efficacy 不夠 / cost / stability issues）

### 同 [[Sumitomo 2020 — Dupilumab Reverses Hair Graying (J Dermatol Letter)|Sumitomo Dupilumab]] 對比

| 維度 | Henkel EUK-134/118 patent | Sumitomo 2020 Dupilumab Letter |
|------|--------------------------|--------------------------------|
| **Mechanism** | Topical SOD/catalase mimetic 抗氧化保護 melanocyte | Systemic IL-4Rα block → 緩解 chronic inflammation → melanocyte function 恢復 |
| **Delivery** | Topical（shampoo／conditioner） | Subcutaneous injection q2w |
| **Cost** | 化妝品 ingredient 成本（cents/dose） | ~US$3,800/月 list price |
| **Side-effect profile** | 化妝品級（low risk） | Conjunctivitis 10-20%, HSV/HZV reactivation |
| **Evidence base** | Patent 自述（強 commercial bias）；無公開 RCT | Single case report（low evidence level）|
| **Status** | Ceased patent | Active drug for AD, off-label observation |

→ 兩者都 **promising mechanism，clinical efficacy 未確證**；對使用者而言 Henkel 嘅 topical 路線**風險低、值得試**（如果揾到含 EUK-134 嘅產品）；dupilumab 純為 cosmetic 用唔合理。

### Anti-graying 領域嘅 broader landscape

- **內源 catalase 不足理論**：Wood et al. 2009 *FASEB J* 經典——「Senile hair graying: H₂O₂-mediated oxidative stress affects human hair color」→ 給 SOD/catalase mimetic（如 EUK-134）路線提供 mechanistic foundation
- **PC-KUS（Pseudocatalase）**：vitiligo / 早期 anti-graying 應用，UVB 激活
- **NB-UVB / Excimer**：vitiligo treatment 副 hair repigmentation 觀察
- **JAK 抑制劑**（baricitinib／ruxolitinib）：alopecia areata 治療有時觀察到 hair color 變化（也 hair regrowth）
- **PRP / scalp microneedling**：anecdotal hair color change reports，evidence 弱

### Abstract

> The invention relates to cosmetics containing EUK-134 and/or EUK-118 and at least one cosmetic care substance, as well as the use of EUK-134 and/or EUK-118 for influencing the natural pigmentation process of skin and/or skin appendages.

### Description（核心摘錄）

The invention relates to cosmetic compositions containing EUK-134 and/or EUK-118 and at least one conditioner, as well as the use of EUK-134 and/or EUK-118 for influencing the **natural pigmentation process of skin and/or skin appendages**.

Hair has a psychosocial function that should not be underestimated. They serve as a means of interpersonal communication and are a sign of their own individuality. **Changes, such as the graying, can lead to a massive impairment of the self-confidence** of the person concerned.

**Pigmentation in the hair follicle is controlled by a defined complex set of molecular signals.** Since melanogenesis in gray follicles is obviously influenced, it can be assumed that parts of this network are modified in the gray follicle. A sequelae is the reduction in melanin synthesis, which leads to graying of the follicle.

The complex set of molecular signals affecting melanogenesis includes, among others, the expression of:

- **MCR1**（melanocortin receptor 1）
- **gp100**（protein of the melanosomal membrane）
- **c-kit**（receptor for stem cell factor）
- with α-MSH（alpha-melanocyte stimulating hormone）and SCF（stem cell factor）as ligands

Since these parameters are of essential importance in hair follicle pigmentation, it is advantageous to influence these parameters if the application of a test formulation is intended to **maintain or reactivate melanin synthesis in the hair follicle cells**.

**Active substance EUK-134**: chloro[[2,2'-[1,2-ethanediylbis[(nitrilo-κN)methylidyne]] to [6-methoxyphenolato-κO]]]-manganese; ethyl bisiminoethyl guaiacol manganese chloride; ethyl bisiminoethyl guaiacol manganese complex; 2,2'-[1,2-ethanediylbis(nitrilomethylidyne)bis[6-methoxy]phenol manganese complex. → exhibits **anti-oxidative effect**.

**Active substance EUK-118**: (acetato-κO)[[2,2'-[1,2-ethanediylbis[(nitrilo-κN)-methylidyne]bis[3,5-dimethoxyphenolato-κO]](2-)]manganese. Structurally analogous to EUK-134, also anti-oxidative.

**Prior art reference**: WO 2003/103616 describes the use of DOPAchrom-tautomerase mimic to protect hair follicle melanocytes from cytotoxic agents (especially those associated with oxidative stress: TNF-α, TGF-β, IL-1β, etc.). Among other active substances, **anti-oxidative mimic EUK-8** is mentioned.

**Object of invention**: provide active compounds / combinations / compositions for influencing the natural pigmentation process — particularly in the hair / hair follicle — **without** the described disadvantages of prior-art methods to influence hair color or degree of hair graying, and youthful appearance of the hair. Also needed: agents that improve skin and/or hair appearance/structure beyond pigmentation alone.

**Solution**: cosmetic compositions containing:
a. **EUK-134 and/or EUK-118**
b. **at least one care substance**

The cosmetic contains EUK-134 and/or EUK-118 + care substance in a **physiologically acceptable carrier**.

- **most preferred: 0.001 – 1 wt%**

> Particularly suitable: polymers (preferably cationic and/or amphoteric polymers), plant extracts, vitamins/provitamins/vitamin precursors, carbohydrates, quinones (particularly biquinones, preferably **ubiquinones [CoQ10]**), bisabolol/bisaboloxides, taurine, silicones, furanones, herbal oils, urea/urea derivatives, natural betaines, creatine, ectoine, α-hydroxy carboxylic acid, α-ketocarboxylic acid, β-hydroxycarboxylic acid (or their ester/lactone/salt form), fatty acid triglycerides, flavonoids/isoflavonoids.
