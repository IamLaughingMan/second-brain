# HYGIEIAS — Company Strategy (v3 backbone)

> Locked integrated strategy. Judge-panel winner: **TRANSPARENCY-MAX**. The backbone all v3 deliverables build on.

## Positioning

**Master line:** "The molecule, not the marketing."
**Company descriptor (under the wordmark):** "Supplements, decoded."
**One-liner (PDP / About / Amazon A+):** "Every HYGIEIAS product shows its molecule, its form, its dose, its purity %, its lot-matched COA, and its price per dose — from daily foundations to the NAD+ frontier."

HYGIEIAS is run as a **decoded-commodity house** in The Ordinary / Deciem register — calm, literal, lab-sheet plain, numerate, anti-hype, treats the customer as smart. Transparency is not a feature on the page; it is the **operating system** of the company. The Greek/Hygieia (goddess of health) romance lives ONLY at the masterbrand level; line names and product names stay ruthlessly literal — that literalness IS the transparency.

**THE FIVE DECODES** — the repeatable proof-system stamped on EVERY product (both lines, identical monospace block):
1. **THE MOLECULE** — ingredient + form/salt + exact dose per serving (e.g. "Magnesium Glycinate · 200 mg elemental"). Form is where cheap brands hide quality; naming it IS the flex.
2. **THE PURITY** — assayed purity % as a number (NMN ≥99% beta-form; omega-3 adds TOTOX / Total Oxidation value + exact EPA/DHA mg), never "high quality."
3. **THE PROOF** — lot-matched Certificate of Analysis (COA / 化驗報告) pulled by batch via **/verify**, plus a named third-party certification where held (USP Verified / NSF / Clean Label Project). Never the empty phrase "third-party tested."
4. **THE PRICE** — price-per-dose AND price-per-gram, computed and shown, with a one-line "priced on formulation cost, not marketing" note.
5. **THE REASONING** — a one-line FORM RATIONALE ("glycinate for absorption + GI tolerance, not cheap oxide") and an honest **EVIDENCE GRADE** (Strong / Moderate / Early).

**Three signature, hard-to-copy assets** (the durable moat beyond the copyable COA-on-page mechanic):
- **THE HYGIEIAS TRANSPARENCY STANDARD** — a short, public, versioned company constitution (v1.0) listing the self-imposed rules every SKU must meet (the Five Decodes + no proprietary blends + full label disclosure incl. inactives + COA published before a batch ships). Linked in the footer and on every PDP. Turns transparency from a claim into an auditable promise.
- **EVIDENCE GRADE badge** — we publicly grade our own science and label weak items "Early." Paired with the honesty clause (grafted from Longevity-Halo): **"The research is real but still early — and we'll show you exactly how early."** Self-criticism is the one move hype competitors structurally cannot replicate.
- **/verify** — a house-wide lot-number COA lookup portal (Nordic-Naturals mechanic, across the WHOLE catalog), printed on every label: "Verify this batch at hygieias.com/verify."

**Claims posture (load-bearing).** Structure/function language only; mandatory DSHEA (Dietary Supplement Health and Education Act) disclaimer linked to each claim; no disease / anti-aging / cure language; FTC (Federal Trade Commission) substantiation = competent and reliable RCT-grade (randomized controlled trial) evidence. Transparency presentation is inherently claims-safe BECAUSE it states facts and limits rather than promising outcomes. A claims-review gate sits on every customer-facing asset; the Longevity line is scripted most conservatively.

## Brand Architecture

**A BRANDED HOUSE.** One masterbrand — **HYGIEIAS** — over TWO named collections (lines, not sub-brands, never separate brands):
- **HYGIEIAS Foundations** — daily essentials.
- **HYGIEIAS Longevity** — healthspan / NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) system, home of the NMN flagship.

The deep-blue wordmark and the Hygieia story sit at the masterbrand level and ONLY there. The endorsement device across both lines is the **shared transparency system itself**, not a separate corporate signature.

**What is shared (the transparency engine = the shared infrastructure = the actual moat):**
- One COA / QA (Quality Assurance) program and the **/verify** lot-lookup portal — every SKU, both lines.
- One **Decoded Panel** template (the Five Decodes) — identical monospace spec block on every PDP and every label.
- One published **Transparency Standard** governing all SKUs.
- One design system: navy ink `#1B2A4A`, orange accent `#D9531E`, monospace for all numbers/specs; one bottle/label kit (solves the no-photography constraint — the label TEXT is the only thing that changes, scaling to ~15 SKUs at near-zero design cost).
- One Stack / Regimen Builder spanning both lines.

**Line differentiation = ONE accent device, nothing more:** Foundations = navy-only label; Longevity = navy label + a single orange accent stripe. Same template, same panel — the stripe is the only visual tell, and it signals the two claims/price postures.

**Why branded house (decisive):** for HYGIEIAS the entire moat IS trust/transparency, a compounding asset that concentrates in ONE name (PR, SEO, the COA program, the Transparency Standard, /verify, the Evidence Grade all accrue to HYGIEIAS). A house-of-brands would force you to rebuild trust N times — capital-prohibitive for a startup and self-defeating when trust is the product. Deciem itself pruned 6 brands to ~2 and runs its transparency engine inside ONE brand. The two lines also cross-subsidize trust in both directions: the Longevity halo (radical honesty where everyone overclaims) flows credibility DOWNWARD, and the everyday-verifiable Foundations anchor belief UPWARD.

**The branded-house trade-off (designed for, not ignored):** every SKU inherits HYGIEIAS trust, so one bad batch hits ALL SKUs, and publishing a constitution raises the bar we are judged against. Therefore the per-lot COA, named certification, and full-label promise are not marketing overhead — **they are the product. QA is funded and staffed as the moat from day one.**

**NMN shock-isolation:** because NMN's (Nicotinamide Mononucleotide) US status is volatile (2022 FDA exclusion → 2023 Amazon delist → 29 Sep 2025 FDA reversal, still NDI-gated), NMN is contained as ONE SKU (`L-NMN-300`) inside Longevity, sitting on a stable Foundations revenue base. An NMN re-ban / Amazon delist / NDI (New Dietary Ingredient) problem damages one SKU, not HYGIEIAS.

## Category & Line Architecture

Two lines under one masterbrand, each running the SAME transparency engine at DIFFERENT claims/price postures. The shared **Decoded Panel** is identical on every PDP; only the claims voice and the accent stripe differ.

### FOUNDATIONS — the trust base, retention / recurring-revenue engine

**Purpose:** broad everyday trust + retention. Commodity molecules where the buying decision is purity, dose, price-per-dose, and form — the decoded-commodity wedge in its purest form, and the proven repurchase base (~38% category repurchase; subscription LTV / Lifetime Value $300–600 standard).
**Claims posture:** confident, established RDI (Recommended Daily Intake) structure/function ("supports normal energy metabolism," "supports muscle and nerve function"). Lowest enforcement risk. Mostly old/established ingredients = no NDI burden = **Amazon-safe from day one.**
**Pricing posture:** pure commodity pricing, positioned BETWEEN NOW Foods (cheap but opaque/downmarket) and Thorne/Pure (premium but COA-gated). "Fair price, shown math."

How transparency manifests per product (FORM is the flex — it is where cheap brands hide quality, and most of these are **independently verifiable by the customer**, which is the proof-of-character that underwrites the harder-to-verify Longevity line):
- **Creatine Monohydrate 5 g** — "monohydrate is the only form that matters — ignore the blends"; Creapure (German) reference grade; trivially cheap price-per-gram shown proudly; Evidence Grade: **Strong.**
- **Magnesium Glycinate 200 mg** — DISCLOSE ELEMENTAL mg (most labels hide low elemental %); glycinate vs cheap oxide rationale; honest "low elemental density = multi-capsule dose" note; Evidence Grade: **Strong.**
- **Vitamin D3 + K2 5000 IU** — IU disclosure; D3-vs-D2 and MK-7-vs-MK-4 education; Evidence Grade: **Strong** (D3), **Moderate** (K2 synergy).
- **Omega-3 1000 mg (660 EPA / 330 DHA)** — the transparency-ARBITRAGE SKU: publish TOTOX + exact EPA/DHA mg + heavy-metals COA in a category plagued by oxidation/mislabeling; algae option for vegan; Evidence Grade: **Moderate** (honest: cardiovascular primary-prevention RCTs largely null; triglyceride support solid).
- Later components: **Zinc Bisglycinate 15 mg**, **Methylated B-Complex** (methylfolate/methylcobalamin vs cheap forms is a genuine decoded story).

### LONGEVITY — the halo, acquisition / AOV / margin engine

**Purpose:** higher-AOV (Average Order Value) hero category (~14% CAGR, ~3× industry) led by the NAD+ system. This is an EDUCATION market (56–77% interested, ~20% buy, ~1/3 recognize ingredients) — clinical transparency + plain-language science is the conversion lever, not hype.
**Claims posture:** MOST conservative. "The research is real but still early." NAD+ / cellular-energy support ONLY; never anti-aging/disease. Evidence Grade badge prominent; hardest claims-review gate; each novel active gated on its own NDI + substantiation review.
**Pricing posture:** still fair / no-luxury-markup but justifiably pricier on rare-ingredient cost; "price reflects formulation cost" does the work.

How transparency manifests per product:
- **NMN 300 mg (FLAGSHIP):** ≥99% beta-NMN purity %, beta- vs alpha-form disclosure, enzymatic-vs-chemical synthesis note, lot COA (purity + heavy metals + micro), price-per-gram vs market, NDIN (New Dietary Ingredient Notification)/supplier-coverage statement, and an honest evidence box: "reliably raises NAD+ in humans; functional-outcome trials are small and early." Evidence Grade: **Moderate.**
- **CoQ10 Ubiquinol 100 mg (Kaneka):** ubiquinol-vs-ubiquinone rationale, Kaneka branded callout, dose disclosure, lot COA; honest note that the strongest data is in disease contexts we cannot claim → cellular-energy/heart structure/function only. Evidence Grade: **Moderate.** Seeded early so the halo is not single-molecule-dependent.
- **Urolithin A 500 mg (Mitopure):** mitophagy mechanism in plain language; Mitopure (Amazentis) GRAS/branded form for COA/IP integrity; "trials largely sponsor-funded" honesty note; **do NOT sell uncertified generic — it breaks the spine.** Evidence Grade: **Moderate**, gated.

**Navigation = transparency-first, dual-axis, curated STACKS as the front door (anti-paradox-of-choice).** Do NOT dump SKUs on the homepage.
- Front door = pre-built stacks + a guided Stack Builder (goal in → protocol out → multi-SKU basket).
- Power-user axes underneath: by **LINE**, by **GOAL** (Thorne-style health-need), and — the transparency-max signature — browse **by PROOF** (filter/sort by "USP/NSF certified," "Evidence: Strong," "≥99% purity," "lowest price-per-dose"). Making transparency a navigation axis is what distinguishes this from a generic spine.
- Hero bridge bundle: **The Healthspan Stack (Foundation Stack + NMN)** — the literal "keep your base, add the frontier" expression that pulls the foundational base into the higher-margin Longevity upsell.

## SKU Naming & Numbering System

**Two layers.** The consumer NAME is literal and human; the SKU CODE is operational and rides the monospace lab aesthetic + drives COA/lot lookup. The literalness of the name is itself the front-line transparency expression.

**LAYER 1 — CONSUMER-FACING NAME = Ingredient + Form/Salt + Dose-per-serving (+ format if needed).**
The name IS the spec sheet, IS the search/AI retrieval anchor, IS the differentiator on a uniform label. No invented hero names, no acronyms-as-names (the anti-NIOD rule). Always disclose FORM and, for minerals, ELEMENTAL dose.

**LAYER 2 — OPERATIONAL SKU CODE = [Line]-[Ingredient]-[Dose].**
Line prefix: **F** = Foundations, **L** = Longevity, **S** = Stack. Ingredient = 3–5 char mnemonic. Dose = numeric in the label's unit. The COA panel appends the lot — the key into /verify. Code is for catalog order, batch/COA lookup, and shelf logic only — never the primary consumer name.

**Worked examples:**

| Consumer name | SKU code | COA-panel form (with lot) |
|---|---|---|
| NMN 300 mg (≥99% beta) | `L-NMN-300` | `L-NMN-300 · Lot 2406A` |
| Creatine Monohydrate 5 g (Creapure) | `F-CREA-5000` | `F-CREA-5000 · Lot 2406A` |
| Magnesium Glycinate 200 mg (elemental) | `F-MAG-200` | `F-MAG-200 · Lot 2407B` |
| The Healthspan Stack (Foundation Stack + NMN) | `S-HEALTHSPAN` | n/a (bundle of member COAs) |

Lot convention: **YYMM + run letter** (`2406A` = 2024-06, batch A).

**The ~15-line system (codes are stable; doses/forms can extend):**

11 active SKUs + 4 parked = a 15-line system. **Adding SKU #16 = fill-in-the-template, not a redesign.**

**NAMING CONSTITUTION (one page, part of the Transparency Standard).** Every new SKU must: (a) name = Ingredient + Form + Dose; (b) belong to exactly ONE line; (c) get an F/L/S code; (d) use the shared label template + Decoded Panel + lot COA; (e) ship ALL required data fields BEFORE it can launch — purity %, elemental/EPA-DHA where relevant, form rationale, price-per-dose AND price-per-gram, Evidence Grade, named cert if held, DSHEA/FTC-reviewed copy, and its COA posted to /verify. Prune ruthlessly; resist needless form/dose proliferation (the Deciem lesson).

## Product Roadmap

Phased to BOTH regulatory/NDI risk AND the "earn trust on boring things, then spend it on the hero" sequencing. **NMN ships first (locked), flanked by high-evidence foundationals** — neither buried as a mere upsell nor over-exposed as the sole front-of-brand. The BRAND leads with the transparency engine; ACQUISITION and ad spend lead on the Amazon-safe, NDI-free foundationals (grafted risk-robust go-to-market), so the foundationals fund cash flow and the QA/COA moat while the NMN halo provides the loud launch narrative.

### PHASE 0 — Pre-launch gates (hard blockers; the infrastructure IS the launch)
- **NMN:** secure a company-specific NDIN OR contract a supplier who holds one (self-affirmed GRAS does NOT suffice). Keep a documented pre-2022 "marketed as a supplement" evidence trail. Source ≥99% beta-NMN with per-batch COAs.
- **Stand up /verify + publish Transparency Standard v1.0 on day one** — they ARE the launch, not a follow-on.
- **Re-verify Amazon's current NMN listing policy** before committing FBA/MCF (Fulfillment by Amazon / Multi-Channel Fulfillment). Contingency: Shopify DTC carries NMN; Amazon carries the Foundations line if NMN listing stays patchy.
- Design labels now for the **FDA Ingredient Traceability Code** labeling requirement (Oct 2026).
- Stand up the claims-review gate (structure/function only, disclaimer linked to each claim, FDA 30-day notification per claim, FTC RCT-grade substantiation).

### WAVE 1 — Build trust around the hero (launch)
**`L-NMN-300` NMN 300 mg (flagship)** + **`F-CREA-5000` Creatine** + **`F-MAG-200` Magnesium Glycinate** + **`F-D3K2-5000` Vitamin D3+K2**, fronted by **`S-NAD`**, **`S-FOUND`**, and the **`S-HEALTHSPAN`** bridge bundle.
- *Why:* pairs the speculative hero with the three highest-evidence, lowest-risk, cheapest, Amazon-safe foundationals (creatine is the single best fit for the spine on all four axes). The customer can independently verify creatine/elemental-magnesium honesty — that proof-of-character underwrites the harder-to-verify NMN Evidence Grade. Launch narrative leads with the halo: "we did the hardest thing — radical transparency where everyone overclaims."
- *Evidence/claims-safety:* Creatine/Mag/D3 = **Strong/Strong/Strong**, structure/function, LOW risk. NMN = **Moderate**, NAD+/cellular-energy support ONLY, "real but still early," highest claims-gate — and lawful again post 29 Sep 2025 but on volatile ground, hence the gates above.

### WAVE 2 — Transparency arbitrage + safe longevity
**`F-OMG3-1000` Omega-3 (TOTOX + exact EPA/DHA)** + **`L-UBQL-100` CoQ10 Ubiquinol (Kaneka)**. Completes `S-FOUND`; `S-NAD` goes live.
- *Why:* Omega-3 is the strongest transparency-arbitrage play — the category is dirty (oxidation, mislabeling, heavy metals), so publishing TOTOX + exact mg + COA is a pure credibility win that showcases the engine and deepens the repurchase base. Ubiquinol is the best-evidenced, safest second Longevity SKU and ensures the halo is **not single-molecule-dependent** (grafted from Longevity-Halo).
- *Evidence/claims-safety:* Omega-3 = **Moderate** (honest: triglyceride support solid, CV primary-prevention null; no heart-disease-prevention claims). CoQ10 = **Moderate**, cellular-energy/heart structure/function only, LOW-MODERATE risk, no IP trap.

### WAVE 3 — Evidence/COGS-gated expansion + low-cost components
**`L-UROA-500` Urolithin A (Mitopure ONLY)** + **`F-ZINC-15` Zinc Bisglycinate** + **`F-BCMX-1` Methylated B-Complex**.
- *Why:* Urolithin A only if Mitopure licensing economics work — never uncertified generic (IP infringement + no GRAS/COA breaks the spine). Zinc and methylated B round out Foundations with genuine decoded stories (elemental mg; methylfolate/methylcobalamin vs cheap forms) at low AOV.
- *Evidence/claims-safety:* Urolithin A = **Moderate** (~12% muscle-strength/endurance signal, but trials sponsor-funded; muscle/cellular-energy structure/function; GRAS helps), COGS/IP-gated. Zinc/B = **Good/Solid**, LOW risk.

### PARKED (re-evaluate as data/IP matures — holding these back IS the spine working)
- **NR / Niagen** — cleanest regulatory path (two NDINs + GRAS) BUT ChromaDex license-or-litigate IP lock-in, the exact trap NMN-first was chosen to avoid; revisit only as a licensed second NAD+ SKU and evidence hedge.
- **Resveratrol** — WEAK human evidence (~2% bioavailability); the classic NMN upsell, but leading with it would betray the spine — a litmus test of whether HYGIEIAS walks its talk. If ever offered, labeled honestly as "Early, low bioavailability."
- **Spermidine** — Early + a real wheat-allergen labeling hazard.
- **Fisetin** — Too early; the tempting senolytic/anti-aging framing is exactly what DSHEA/FTC forbid.

**Commercial model:** one-time purchase AND subscription (never subscription-only — Ritual's friction, Care/of's death under Bayer). Foundationals = the subscribe-and-save LTV engine; Longevity = higher-AOV acquisition. Engineer onboarding against the 20–30% first-month churn cliff with education-led, transparency-reinforcing onboarding to convert trial into the 5–8% steady-state cohort. Target **≥3:1 LTV:CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost). Monetize NMN's high AOV/margin/PR pull to fund the per-batch testing and house-wide COA infrastructure the engine requires.

## Strategic Rationale & Risks

**Why this is the locked strategy.** Of the three angles, **TRANSPARENCY-MAX** is the only one that leads the BRAND with the locked moat itself (the transparency engine) rather than picking a line to lead — which satisfies the NMN-first lock cleanly (NMN ships Wave 1 as flagship, flanked by high-evidence foundationals). It banks the hardest-to-copy assets: the versioned public **Transparency Standard**, house-wide **/verify**, **browse-by-PROOF** navigation, and above all the **EVIDENCE GRADE** — self-grading your own science is a culturally-hard move hype competitors structurally cannot replicate, and the one differentiator that does NOT reduce to the copyable COA-on-page mechanic. It is also the most claims-safe: stating facts + honest limits is inherently safer than promising outcomes.

It occupies a genuine, uncontested white space: no broad branded house puts a lot-matched COA + purity % + price-per-dose on EVERY PDP across BOTH a foundational and a longevity tier (Thorne/Pure/Life Extension/NOW gate or omit COAs; Ritual is foundational-only sourcing-transparency; the NMN natives are longevity-only). It is on-trend, not contrarian (>80% of buyers cite transparency/traceability as decisive). It solves the constraints: a typographic lab-sheet system needs zero photography and scales to ~15 SKUs at near-zero design cost; facts and honest limits are inherently DSHEA/FTC-safe. And it compounds in a branded house — every reusable asset accrues to one name.

**The grafts that make it win.** From **Foundational-Trust**: proof-of-character sequencing (Wave 1 foregrounds cheap, independently-verifiable foundationals so customers confirm honesty where verification is easy, underwriting the NMN Evidence Grade); risk-robust go-to-market (lead acquisition/ad spend on Amazon-safe, NDI-free foundationals to fund the QA moat and contain NMN's blast radius); the **Healthspan Stack** bridge bundle; subscribe-and-save LTV engineering against the churn cliff. From **Longevity-Halo**: the loud launch narrative (radical transparency where everyone overclaims, credibility flowing downward); the honesty clause paired with the Evidence Grade on every Longevity PDP; seeding non-NMN longevity SKUs (CoQ10 in Wave 2); and monetizing the hero's AOV/PR to pay for the moat.

**Risks (designed-for, not ignored):**
- **NMN regulatory tail-risk** — the 29 Sep 2025 reversal is an explicit "mixed bag"; litigation expected, post-Loper-Bright re-reversal possible, CA private litigation/Prop-65 exposure remains. *Mitigation:* NMN is ONE SKU on a stable Foundations base; NDIN + pre-2022 evidence trail; foundationals can carry Amazon alone.
- **NDI exposure** — NMN (and later urolithin A, spermidine) require a company-specific NDIN or a supplier who filed; self-affirmed GRAS does not suffice. Launching without it is a hard stop.
- **Amazon channel risk** — historically delisted, listings patchy; re-verify before committing FBA/MCF; Shopify DTC contingency.
- **The moat is also a single point of failure** — in a branded house one bad batch / missed COA / over-optimistic grade hits ALL SKUs and contradicts the published Standard; reputational whiplash is amplified. *Mitigation:* QA funded as the product, not overhead; COA posted before any batch ships.
- **Self-imposed accountability backfire** — publishing a constitution + grades raises the bar you are judged against. *Mitigation:* never ship a batch before its COA posts; grade conservatively.
- **DSHEA/FTC drift on Longevity** — the more "scientific" the presentation, the easier to imply disease/anti-aging claims; highest enforcement-risk area. *Mitigation:* mandatory claims-review gate on every asset, structure/function only, RCT-grade substantiation.
- **Margin compression** — thin-GM% foundational commodities + visible price-per-dose caps markup. *Mitigation:* lean on Longevity AOV, subscription LTV, and a USP/NSF certification premium (20–40%) to fund QA and branded reference grades (Creapure, Kaneka, Mitopure).
- **Operational cost/complexity** — per-batch third-party testing + a house-wide lot-COA portal is real infrastructure and headcount from day one. This is locked for all three angles anyway (the moat is trust); maximizing it is the right capital allocation, not waste.
- **Copyability** — COA-on-page is technically copyable by incumbents; durable differentiation depends on being first, doing it house-wide as identity, AND the culturally-hard Evidence Grade — not the COA mechanic alone.
- **Paradox of choice** — a spec-dense 15-SKU catalog can paralyze beginners; if navigation ever reverts to dumping SKUs instead of leading with curated Stacks + Stack Builder, the transparency-max presentation actively hurts conversion.
- **Cross-sell bridge is an assumption** — Longevity-acquired buyers must convert into foundational subscribers; instrument the cross-sell rate via the Healthspan Stack + subscribe-and-save early.
