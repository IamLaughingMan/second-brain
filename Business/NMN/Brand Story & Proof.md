# HYGIEIAS — Brand Story & Proof Inventory

> Methodology 步驟 3。品牌故事 = 客向英文 copy(US 市場);proof = 信任底料盤點。

## 品牌名 HYGIEIAS
- 源自 **Hygieia(Ὑγίεια)**,希臘健康女神 —— 藥房「蛇杖碗(Bowl of Hygieia)」標誌出處。
- 象徵 **預防 / 保養**(非治療)→ 完美對應 NMN healthspan 定位 **且 claims-safe**(避 disease claim)。
- 「Hygieia → hygiene → 純淨」扣返純度 / COA 透明敘事。
- **Logo:** 深藍 wordmark「HYGIEIAS」→ 坐實 [[Brand & Web Design Brief|Direction A]] clinical-blue 調色(以 logo 深藍作 primary ink/accent)。

## Brand Story（English — customer-facing draft v1）

**Tagline 候選:** "Health, decoded." · "Proof, not promises." · "The molecule, not the marketing."

**Positioning one-liner:**
> High-purity, third-party-tested NMN — with the proof on the label and none of the markup.

**About (long):**

> HYGIEIAS takes its name from Hygieia, the Greek goddess of health — the figure behind the bowl-and-serpent that still marks pharmacies today. She didn't stand for the cure. She stood for prevention: the daily practice of staying well.
>
> Most NMN is sold one of two ways — wrapped in anti-aging promises no supplement can legally make, or priced like a luxury because someone has to pay for the marketing. Neither felt honest.
>
> NMN is a single, well-defined molecule. What matters isn't the story on the bottle — it's the purity, the dose, and whether an independent lab can confirm both. So that's what we sell: NMN we can document, made in a cGMP facility, from an ingredient that meets the FDA's New Dietary Ingredient requirements, and third-party tested with a report you can read before you buy.
>
> We don't promise to reverse your age. We tell you the milligrams, the purity, and the price per gram — then let you decide.
>
> Serious nutrition for people who'd rather read the data than the hype.

> ⚠️ **Claims-safe 已內建**:明寫「we don't promise to reverse your age」、框成 prevention/data;**禁** disease/anti-aging 療效字眼。上線前過 DSHEA + FTC 文案 review。
