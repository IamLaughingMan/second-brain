# NMN — Offer Architecture (flagship)

> **HYGIEIAS Longevity · the flagship launch.** "The molecule, not the marketing." Every figure below traces to the first-batch facts on file, or is explicitly marked **To confirm** / benchmark. Claims posture: NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) / cellular-energy structure-function support ONLY — no anti-aging, no disease claims; "the research is real but still early." **Evidence grade: Emerging (early-stage human research).** Safety supported in healthy adults (Fukamizu et al., 2022, *Scientific Reports*); efficacy still limited — a 2024 systematic review of randomized trials (Wen et al., *Cureus*) found most physical-performance outcomes **not statistically significant**. Frame as prevention + data, never as outcomes.

---

## 1. SKU lineup & roles

NMN launches in **two formats of one molecule** — same ≥99% beta-NMN, same lot-matched COA (Certificate of Analysis) program, same orange Longevity accent stripe. The "one-SKU shock-isolation" principle is about the *molecule's* regulatory blast radius, not format count: both formats roll up under the single NMN ingredient line, so an NMN-specific shock (re-ban / Amazon delist / NDI problem) is still contained to one ingredient on a stable Foundations base.

| Role | Consumer name | SKU code | Format / supply | Dose | One-time price |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Convenience hero / acquisition anchor** | NMN 300 mg (≥99% beta) — Capsules | `L-NMN-300` | 60 caps · 18 g · ~60-day supply (1/day) | 300 mg/day | **$34.95** |
| **Value hero / power-user** | NMN 500 mg/serving (≥99% beta) — Powder | `L-NMN-500` | 100 g · ~200 servings · ~6.6-month supply | 500 mg/day | **$44.95** |

`L-NMN-500` code = proposed extension of the locked `[Line]-[Ingredient]-[Dose]` convention; consistent with `L-NMN-300`. **To confirm** as the catalog code.

**Roles, decisively:**

- **`L-NMN-300` Capsule — the default front door.** Highest gross margin (80.4%), lowest price of entry, 1/day convenience, the cleanest Amazon/FBA item, and the natural **subscription anchor** (60-day supply = one cycle). This is the SKU acquisition and ad spend point to, and the one the Healthspan/NAD+ stacks are built around. The 300 mg/day dose is informed by 2022 human *safety* research in healthy adults (Fukamizu et al., *Scientific Reports*; 66 participants, 60 days) — a safety, not efficacy, finding. **To confirm:** that the trial's exact daily dose matches our 300 mg label (the source data bundles them; verify the published dose before making any "matches-the-trial" claim).
- **`L-NMN-500` Powder — the value flex and AOV (Average Order Value) lifter.** Carries the **hero price-per-gram number ($0.38–0.45/g)** that beats the entire competitor set, embodies "molecule, not marketing," and appeals to the biohacker/power-user cohort dosing 500 mg/day. Higher basket ($44.95) but lower GM% (54.4%) and a ~6.6-month repurchase cycle, so it is less subscription-frequent — sell it as a one-time value statement and an upsell from the capsule.

**Upsell paths (the front door is a stack, not a SKU dump):**
- `S-NAD` — The NAD+ Protocol (NMN + CoQ10 Ubiquinol) — **Wave 2**, pricing **To confirm**.
- `S-HEALTHSPAN` — The Healthspan Stack (Foundation Stack + NMN) — **Wave 1 bridge bundle**, the "keep your base, add the frontier" upsell that pulls Foundations buyers into the higher-margin Longevity line. Pricing **To confirm**.

**Read on the model:** the capsule is the margin/cash engine (80.4% GM, 61% contribution) and the right SKU to scale; the powder trades margin for the headline price-per-gram and AOV. The capsule alone covers its fulfillment and product cost with room to fund the QA/COA moat; the powder's thinner contribution (36%) means it should be positioned as a value statement and bundle component rather than a paid-acquisition target.

## 4. Subscribe & save

- **Program: subscribe & save 15%, both formats**, free shipping, cancel anytime — never subscription-only (the Ritual friction / Care-of cautionary tale).
- Capsule: $34.95 → **$29.95** (effective −14.3%, rounded to the .95 price point); price/gram $1.94 → **$1.66**.
- Powder: $44.95 → **$38.20** (−15.0%); price/gram $0.45 → **$0.38**.
- **Anchor on the capsule.** Its 60-day supply maps to a clean monthly/bi-monthly cadence and the highest retained margin (77.1% GM at sub price). This is the LTV (Lifetime Value) engine for the flagship.
- The powder's ~6.6-month cycle makes it a poor subscription frequency — offer subscribe-and-save for completeness, but do not engineer the funnel around it.
- Engineer onboarding against the category-typical first-month churn cliff (commonly cited **~20–30%**, **To confirm** — not in our data) with education-led, transparency-reinforcing onboarding (what NAD+ is, what the research does and doesn't show) to convert trial into a steady-state retained cohort (benchmark **~5–8%**, **To confirm**). Planning target **≥3:1 LTV:CAC**.

## 5. Guarantee & shipping

Decisive defaults (dollar thresholds **To confirm** against the final Amazon fulfillment quote):

- **60-day satisfaction (money-back) guarantee.** Matches one full capsule cycle and is claims-safe — it promises *satisfaction*, not an *outcome*. (Competitor guarantee terms cited for benchmarking — Renue By Science ~60-day, ProHealth ~100-day — are **To confirm**; not in our price-check data.)
- **Free US shipping over $35** (so a single powder and any subscription ship free; a single one-time capsule sits just under, nudging toward subscribe or a stack). **To confirm** the threshold once per-unit fulfillment is quoted.
- **Subscriptions always ship free.**
- Claims-safe guarantee copy: satisfaction/quality guarantee only — no efficacy or health-outcome promise.

## 7. Fulfillment (Amazon MCF / FBA)

- **Engine:** Amazon **MCF (Multi-Channel Fulfillment)** for Shopify DTC orders; **FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon)** if/when listed on Amazon.
- **Per-unit estimates (TBD — awaiting Amazon dimension quote):** capsule ~**$5–6**, powder ~**$6–7**. These drive the contribution line in §3 and the shipping-threshold decision in §5; lock them before finalizing the price ladder economics. (Note: the §3 on-file contribution figures imply higher fulfillment — ~$6.8 / ~$8.2 — than this range; reconcile.)
- **Channel sequencing for the flagship:** Shopify DTC is the primary, regulatory-resilient home for NMN (carries the SKU if Amazon listing stays patchy). NMN **returned to Amazon 2025-12**, so FBA is viable again — but **re-verify Amazon's current NMN listing policy before committing FBA/MCF inventory** (historical delisting risk). Contingency: Shopify carries NMN; Amazon carries the Foundations line if NMN listing turns unstable.
- **Phase-0 hard gate, fulfillment-linked:** the lot-matched COA must be posted to **/verify before any batch ships**, and every label must carry lot coding that maps to its posted COA. *(The draft's "FDA Ingredient Traceability Code — Oct 2026" requirement is **not in our data and could not be verified** — likely a confusion with FSMA 204 food-traceability rules, which target the Food Traceability List rather than dietary supplements broadly, and carry different dates. **To confirm** the actual labeling/traceability obligation against current FDA cGMP rules before treating it as a gate.)*
